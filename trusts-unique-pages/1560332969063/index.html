What happens next

We will post John Smith a Unique Taxpayer Reference (UTR). If they are based in the UK, this can take 15 working days. For international trustees, this can take up to 21 working days.

What you need to do next

Make a note of your reference number in case you need to contact HMRC. If you do not get your UTR within 15 working days, contact HMRC to find out why (opens in a new window or tab).

You cannot make online changes to the trust. To change the lead trustee or their contact details, post the changes to HMRC (opens in a new window or tab). You must keep a record of any other changes until you can change them using the online service.
